## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text

**Description:** Uses text-based evaluation across five languages (Filipino, Indonesian, Tamil, Thai, Vietnamese) and English to evaluate language and cultural understanding.

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** linguistic analysis, cultural commonsense reasoning, sentiment analysis, safety alignment evaluation, translation

**Detailed Description:** Evaluates models on classical NLP tasks (sentiment, translation), linguistic nuances unique to Southeast Asian languages, cultural knowledge QA, and localized safety alignments.

## Summary

The paper presents SEA-HELM, a holistic linguistic and cultural evaluation suite tailored for Southeast Asian languages, spanning Filipino, Indonesian, Tamil, Thai, and Vietnamese. It structures evaluation across five pillars: NLP Classics, LLM-Specifics, SEA Linguistics, SEA Culture, and Safety, accompanied by a public leaderboard.

## Key Findings

Establishes a systematic benchmark showing that while mainstream LLMs perform well on classic NLP tasks, they exhibit significant drops in performance when faced with localized SEA linguistics and regional cultural contexts.
