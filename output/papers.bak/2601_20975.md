## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text

**Description:** Evaluates agents using natural language prompts to browse, retrieve, and synthesize text-based information from open web documents and databases.

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** Structured Retrieval, Context Management, Logical Reasoning, Entity Resolution, Constraint Solving

**Detailed Description:** Requires multi-step dependency-graph search, entity de-duplication across diverse unstructured web sources, and logical reasoning under epistemic uncertainty to solve complex constraints and determine optimal search stopping criteria.

## Summary

DeepSearchQA is a 900-prompt benchmark designed to evaluate autonomous web agents on difficult multi-step information-seeking tasks. Moving beyond single-answer verification, it tests three complex recall-oriented capabilities: systematic collation of fragmented information, entity resolution, and reasoning about stopping criteria. Performance is evaluated strictly on the precision and completeness (recall) of the submitted answer sets.

## Key Findings

Gemini Deep Research and GPT-5 Pro High Reasoning establish the state-of-the-art with F1 scores of 81.90% and 78.98% respectively, but show a 13-15% gap below their strict 'Fully Correct' success rates (~65-66%). Below a certain parameter and compute threshold, agent performance drops precipitously, indicating a hard reasoning floor for autonomous deep research.
