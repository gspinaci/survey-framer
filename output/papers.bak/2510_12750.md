## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** instance identity, instance attribute, instance location, instance counting, spatial relation, instance interaction, visual-inspired reasoning

**Detailed Description:** Evaluates foundational visual perception and advanced iconographic, spatial, and causal reasoning on art.

## Summary

The paper presents VQArt-Bench, a visual question answering dataset of 14,463 questions on art history and cultural heritage. It utilizes a collaborative multi-agent LLM pipeline to generate high-quality, linguistically diverse multiple-choice questions.

## Key Findings

Gemini 2.5 achieved the highest overall accuracy (71%), followed by Kimi-VL (67%) as the top open-source model. All models showed a notable weakness in instance counting despite stronger performance in higher-level visual reasoning.
