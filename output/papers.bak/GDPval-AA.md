## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text, images, documents, spreadsheets, slides, multimedia

**Description:** Evaluates models on complex multi-modal tasks where instructions and reference files are provided, and outputs can range from text documents and presentations to spreadsheets, diagrams, and multimedia.

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** professional drafting, editing, media production planning, domain-specific reasoning, concept design

**Detailed Description:** Evaluates performance on complex real-world deliverables such as legal brief writing, video editing instructions, engineering conceptualization, and financial analysis.

## Summary

GDPval is a benchmark developed by OpenAI to measure LLM performance on 1,320 economically valuable, real-world knowledge work tasks across 44 occupations in 9 industries. The tasks are derived from real-world deliverables and graded blindly by experienced human professionals and an automated grader.

## Key Findings

Frontier models are starting to approach human expert quality on professional tasks; Claude Opus 4.1 performed best, producing outputs rated as good as or better than humans in nearly half of the tasks. Performance more than tripled from GPT-4o to GPT-5, and models completed tasks roughly 100x faster and 100x cheaper than human experts (excluding human oversight and iteration).
