## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text

**Description:** Text-only multiple-choice questions formatted in natural language and LaTeX.

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** multiple-choice question answering, factual knowledge retrieval, philosophical reasoning, historical context analysis, literary interpretation

**Detailed Description:** Evaluates graduate-level understanding and multi-step reasoning, requiring models to analyze complex textual statements, retrieve domain-specific scholarly knowledge, and solve reasoning tasks with multiple plausible distractors.

## Summary

SuperGPQA is a large-scale evaluation benchmark designed to test LLMs across 285 graduate-level disciplines using 26,529 text-based multiple-choice questions. The benchmark features a robust filtering pipeline involving human-LLM collaboration to eliminate trivial or ambiguous questions, resulting in high-difficulty items with up to 10 options per question.

## Key Findings

Reasoning models like DeepSeek-R1 and OpenAI o1 achieve the best performance, with DeepSeek-R1 leading at 61.82% accuracy. Humanities disciplines like History and Law demonstrated much higher discrimination power (standard deviations of 8.45 and 7.17 respectively) than STEM disciplines, indicating that humanities are highly effective at exposing performance variance among SOTA models.
