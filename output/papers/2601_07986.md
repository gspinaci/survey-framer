## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text, image

**Description:** Vision-language evaluation combining artwork images with expert-written bilingual critiques. Models generate art criticism text based on visual analysis of cultural artworks.

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** cultural art critique generation, hierarchical cultural understanding, cross-cultural aesthetic interpretation, symbolic meaning analysis, philosophical aesthetics reasoning

**Detailed Description:** Five-layer framework (L1-L5) from visual perception to philosophical aesthetics. Tasks require progressively deeper cultural knowledge: visual recognition (L1), technical analysis (L2), cultural symbolism (L3), historical context (L4), and philosophical aesthetics (L5). Evaluated through dimension coverage of culture-specific concepts.

## Summary

VULCA-BENCH introduces a comprehensive multicultural art critique benchmark with 7,410 image-critique pairs across 8 cultural traditions, operationalized through a five-layer cultural understanding framework (L1-L5) with 225 culture-specific dimensions. The benchmark addresses gaps in existing VLM evaluation by measuring hierarchical cultural interpretation beyond surface-level visual perception.

## Key Findings

All evaluated VLMs show a consistent 25-40 percentage-point performance drop from visual perception (L1-L2) to cultural interpretation (L3-L5), with Gemini-2.5-Pro achieving highest DCR of 72.4% and GLM-4V-Flash lowest at 58.2%. Error analysis reveals systematic failures in surface-level terminology use, historical anachronism, and cultural conflation.
