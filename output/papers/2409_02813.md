## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text, image

**Description:** Multi-modal evaluation combining visual and textual information, with a special 'vision-only' setting where questions are embedded within images/screenshots that models must read and interpret

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** multimodal reasoning, visual question answering, complex problem solving, domain-specific knowledge application

**Detailed Description:** College-level analytical tasks requiring integration of visual and textual information across disciplines, with emphasis on true multimodal understanding rather than exploiting shortcuts

## Summary

MMMU-Pro introduces a more robust version of the MMMU benchmark that filters text-only solvable questions, augments answer options to 10 choices, and includes a vision-only input setting where questions are embedded in images. The benchmark aims to evaluate true multimodal understanding by preventing models from exploiting shortcuts and requiring genuine integration of visual and textual information.

## Key Findings

Performance drops significantly across all models compared to original MMMU, ranging from 16.8% to 26.9% decreases. GPT-4o achieved 51.9% on MMMU-Pro vs 69.1% on original MMMU. Chain-of-thought prompting generally improves performance, while OCR prompts show minimal impact. The vision-only setting particularly challenges models' ability to seamlessly integrate visual and textual information.
