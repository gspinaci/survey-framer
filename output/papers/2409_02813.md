## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text, image

**Description:** Multi-modal evaluation combining text questions with images, including a vision-only setting where questions are embedded within screenshots/photos requiring models to process both modalities simultaneously

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** visual question answering, multimodal reasoning, domain knowledge application, cross-modal understanding

**Detailed Description:** College-level questions requiring integration of visual and textual information across multiple disciplines, with emphasis on genuine multimodal understanding rather than shortcut exploitation

## Summary

MMMU-Pro introduces a more robust version of the MMMU benchmark that filters out text-only solvable questions, augments candidate options from 4 to 10, and introduces a vision-only input setting where questions are embedded in images. The benchmark reveals significant performance drops across all tested models, with decreases ranging from 16.8% to 26.9% compared to original MMMU.

## Key Findings

All models showed substantial performance drops on MMMU-Pro compared to MMMU, with GPT-4o dropping from 69.1% to 51.9% overall. The vision-only setting proved particularly challenging, with OCR prompts showing minimal improvement. Chain of Thought reasoning helped in technical domains but was less effective in subjective areas like Art and Design. Error analysis showed reasoning errors increased from 26% to 46% of failures.
