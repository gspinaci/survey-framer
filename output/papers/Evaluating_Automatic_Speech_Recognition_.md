## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** automatic speech recognition

**Detailed Description:** Transcription of spontaneous, emotional, accented, and multilingual historical speech from oral history archives, evaluated using Word Error Rate (WER) across eight different languages.

## Summary

This paper presents the first large-scale evaluation of OpenAI's Whisper automatic speech recognition model on the Fortunoff Video Archive of Holocaust testimonies. The study systematically assesses Whisper's transcription performance across eight languages, highlighting the challenges of applying off-the-shelf speech technologies to highly sensitive, accented, and emotional historical oral histories.

## Key Findings

Provides a comprehensive linguistic and technical analysis of Whisper's performance on spontaneous testimony speech across eight languages, identifying crucial bottlenecks such as code-switching, emotional expressions, and non-native accents.
