## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text

**Description:** Text-only multiple-choice questions with up to 10 options, translated to English where necessary. Multimodal inputs (like diagrams or images) are explicitly filtered out during the quality inspection phase to ensure a clean text-based QA format.

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** multiple-choice question answering, factual recall, logical reasoning, domain-specific knowledge retrieval, statement verification

**Detailed Description:** Graduate-level multiple-choice question answering (MCQA) involving factual recall, conceptual understanding, historical and aesthetic statement verification, and complex reasoning across specialized academic disciplines.

## Summary

SuperGPQA is a comprehensive evaluation benchmark designed to probe the limits of LLM capabilities across 285 graduate-level disciplines, including key arts and humanities domains. Using a collaborative human-LLM filtering framework, it curates a dataset of 26,529 highly difficult multiple-choice questions to challenge state-of-the-art reasoning models.

## Key Findings

Reasoning models like DeepSeek-R1 and OpenAI o1 achieved the highest overall accuracies (~61.8% and ~60.2% respectively), demonstrating significant remaining climbing space. Instruction tuning provides a massive performance boost over base models. Interestingly, humanities disciplines like History and Law demonstrated the highest discrimination power among top-performing models.
