## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text

**Description:** Text-only benchmark consisting of multiple-choice questions (MCQs) across 11 Indic languages covering both general and culturally specific knowledge

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** knowledge_assessment, cultural_understanding, multilingual_reasoning

**Detailed Description:** Multiple-choice questions testing factual knowledge and cultural understanding across domains like Arts & Humanities, Social Sciences, Law & Governance, alongside STEM subjects, requiring culturally-aware reasoning in low-resource languages

## Summary

MILU introduces a comprehensive evaluation benchmark for Large Language Models across 11 Indic languages, spanning 8 domains and 41 subjects with culturally relevant content from Indian regional exams. The benchmark reveals that current LLMs struggle with culturally specific knowledge, with GPT-4o achieving the highest accuracy of 74%.

## Key Findings

GPT-4o achieved highest performance at 74% average accuracy. Open multilingual models outperformed language-specific fine-tuned models. Models performed significantly better on high-resource languages vs low-resource ones, and struggled most with culturally relevant domains like Arts & Humanities and Law & Governance compared to STEM subjects.
