## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text

**Description:** Text-only multiple choice questions in 11 Indic languages covering culturally specific topics from regional examinations

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** multiple choice question answering, cross-lingual evaluation, cultural knowledge assessment

**Detailed Description:** Cultural knowledge evaluation requiring understanding of region-specific history, arts, festivals, laws, and traditions across Indian states; tests both linguistic competence and cultural understanding beyond simple factual recall

## Summary

MILU introduces the first comprehensive benchmark for evaluating LLMs on culturally relevant knowledge in 11 Indic languages, covering 8 domains and 41 subjects with questions sourced from regional Indian examinations. The benchmark specifically tests cultural understanding of local history, arts, festivals, and laws rather than just linguistic capability.

## Key Findings

GPT-4o achieved highest accuracy at 74%. Open multilingual models outperformed language-specific fine-tuned models. Models performed significantly worse in culturally relevant domains (Arts & Humanities, Social Sciences) compared to STEM fields, highlighting lack of cultural knowledge in current models.
