## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text

**Description:** Text-only multiple-choice questions covering 41 subjects across 8 domains in 11 Indic languages

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** multiple-choice question answering, multilingual understanding, cultural knowledge assessment, domain-specific reasoning

**Detailed Description:** Evaluates factual knowledge and reasoning across culturally relevant domains like Arts & Humanities (26.1%), Social Sciences, History, Literature, and Law & Governance, requiring both linguistic competence and cultural understanding

## Summary

MILU introduces a comprehensive multilingual benchmark for evaluating LLMs across 11 Indic languages, spanning 8 domains and 41 subjects with emphasis on culturally relevant knowledge. The benchmark reveals significant performance gaps in culturally specific domains like Arts & Humanities compared to general STEM fields.

## Key Findings

GPT-4o achieved highest performance at 74% average accuracy. Models perform significantly better in high-resource languages and general domains like STEM, but struggle with culturally relevant areas like Arts & Humanities and Social Sciences. Open multilingual models outperformed language-specific fine-tuned models.
