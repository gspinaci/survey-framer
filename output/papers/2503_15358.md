## Benchmark Modalities

**Modality Types:** text, images, image_sequences

**Description:** Multi-modal benchmark combining text (context sentences with idioms), static images (depicting literal/figurative meanings), and temporal image sequences (3-panel comic-like representations). Also provides text-only version using image captions.

## Analytical Tasks

**Task Types:** multimodal_ranking, image_completion, idiomaticity_detection, semantic_alignment

**Detailed Description:** Task A requires ranking images by semantic alignment with idiomatic vs literal meanings in context. Task B involves predicting sequence completion while distinguishing literal/idiomatic senses. Both require deep semantic understanding of figurative language beyond surface-level pattern matching.

## Summary

AdMIRe introduces a multimodal benchmark for evaluating LLMs' understanding of idiomatic expressions through image ranking and sequence completion tasks. The dataset contains 100 English and 55 Portuguese idiomatic compounds with corresponding visual representations of their literal and figurative meanings.

## Key Findings

Top-performing systems achieved human-level performance (93% accuracy) but required substantial computational resources, mixture-of-experts approaches, and careful prompt engineering. LLMs showed bias toward idiomatic interpretations regardless of context, and multimodal approaches generally outperformed text-only methods.
